Can I use my virtual address as my Companies House registered business address?
Yes, your virtual address can act as your official business address and can be used to register with Companies House.

How do I know if you have received some of my post?
If you are a Virtual Office Collect customer, we will email you to let you know that we’ve got some post waiting to be collected. If you are a Virtual Office Forward customer, we will automatically post it straight to you.

How long is a virtual office setup?
We will definitely have you set up at the beginning of the next business day following your order. But our average time is just 10 minutes! (exluding KYC checks)

What about mail addressed to me personally?
We will handle mail sent to your trading name, your registered company name (if different) and your personal name.

Is this offer open to all SMEs or just digital companies?
This service is available to anyone!

Do I need to provide ID?
Yes, Company Directors and Beneficial owners (person of significant control) will need proof of ID – either a passport or driving license and a recent utility bill. An original scan or photograph is sufficient and can be will be authorised via our online ID proof validation software.

What will my new company address be? Your virtual office will have the following address: Company Name, 86-90 Paul Street, London EC2A 4NE.
Does the registered office address need to be the same as the trading address?
You can trade from your registered office address, but a company’s trading address does not have to be the same as their registered office address.

Can I use the virtual office business address on my marketing materials?
Yes, you are free to use your virtual London address across all marketing and communications including letterheads, business cards and your website.
